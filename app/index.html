Retail + Connected Devices = Engaged Customers!

Meet Dani, Owner of Flock

A boutique retail store

Like many Retailers, Flock has a brick and mortar presence in an up-and-coming location in Boston. Additionally, the store has an ecommerce presence that allows customers to purchase the latest styles.

Dani is looking for opportunities to:

Drive Traffic into Store

Dani is continually looking for new ways to drive traffic to Flock, having used traditional online ads and social media, with mixed results. Imagine being able to dynamically target and drive shoppers to specific products in your physical store, in realtime. By proactively identifying customers based on social media activity, brand awareness and location; you can target offers that convert interest into transactions.

Better Understand customers

Dani like most retailers has a knowledgable staff and myriad of data based on tranactions, POS, inventory, loyalty and credit card programs. Unfortunately the staff and the systems can not alway communicate with each other. What if you could leverage this data to provide a tailored buying experience to each unique customer need. By providing a detailed view of your customers behavior in combination with smarter inventory, you can provide customers with what they want to buy and seamlesly manage what is in stock.

Interactive in-store Experience

Dani has invested in creating a unique in-store experience, yet it is still not as interactive and engaging as she would like. What if it's possible for merchandise to interact with customers - telling them what they want to know in order to purchase. Now you can deliver a memorable experience where shoppers interact with your brand; captivating and delighting them to come back.
