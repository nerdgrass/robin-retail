Meet Dani

forward-thinker and owner of Flock

Dani uses technology to solve her problems and she's not doing anything wrong. But she's not doing anything new.

Brick & morter meets social & smart

Flock is a boutique retail store

Like many Retailers, Flock has a brick and mortar presence in an up-and-coming location in Boston. Additionally, the store has an ecommerce presence that allows customers to purchase the latest styles.

Dani is looking for opportunities to:

Bring the right customers in the door

Dani is looking for new ways to get people to come to Flock, and has been using traditional online ads and social media. She is not love with the results. She wants to knows more than just Flock’s social network headcount, and better reach the people behind her likes and followers. Her customers don’t have to be strangers. Knowing how and when to target customers is hard. We get it. Robin gives Dani the ability to learn more about who her customers are, where they are, and when they need to come into her store. From there, she can target her customers with personalized messaging from Flock that is relevant to them at the right time.

Understand her customers better

Dani has access to a lot of different data in her store and also has smart people. Putting the two together is not always clear. What if Dani’s team could use the data she has to teach her store to respond to customers and get them what they want to buy? With Robin in play, she is able to enable Flock to understand its customers as well as Dani does. With a store as smart as Dani is, she can know more about what and who is in her store, and get customers more of what they want.

You have to check out this store!

Dani cares about how her customers experience her store. She wants to bring her brand to life inside Flock and give her customers a unique experience. For her, traditional displays fell flat. With Robin she can build experiences she did not know were possible, until they happen. As a customer walks through Flock and touches the items or display, items respond to customers--showing images of coordinating pieces or complementary colors. The possibilities are endless. Dani is now able to give her customers the wow moments they have to tell their friends about.
